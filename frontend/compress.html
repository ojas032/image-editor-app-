Frequently Asked Questions

What quality level should I use?

For web use, quality 75-85 provides excellent visual quality with significant file size reduction. For print, use 90-95 for maximum quality.

Is image compression really free?

Yes! ImageNerd's image compressor is completely free with no limits, watermarks, or registration required.

Are my images uploaded to a server?

No. All compression happens locally in your browser. Your images never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy and security.

Can I compress multiple images at once?

Currently, you can compress one image at a time. Upload, compress, download, then repeat for additional images.

Will compressed images work for print?

For print, use quality settings above 90 to maintain high resolution. Lower quality settings are better suited for web and digital use.
