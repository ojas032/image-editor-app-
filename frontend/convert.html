Supported Formats

JPEG/JPG

Most common photo format. Excellent compression with adjustable quality. Universal support across all platforms and applications.

PNG

Lossless compression with transparency support. Perfect for graphics, logos, and images that need to maintain quality. Larger file sizes than JPEG.

WebP

Modern format by Google. Superior compression (smaller files) while maintaining quality. Growing support across browsers and platforms.

HEIC

Apple's format for iPhone photos. Excellent compression and quality. Convert to more widely supported formats for sharing.

GIF

Supports animation and transparency. Limited to 256 colors. Good for simple animations and small graphics.

BMP

Uncompressed format. Highest quality but very large file sizes. Used in professional printing and graphics work.

TIFF

Professional format supporting layers and metadata. Lossless compression option. Used in photography and printing industries.
