Privacy Policy

How we protect your privacy and handle your data

Last updated: November 14, 2025

Information We Collect

ImageNerd is committed to protecting your privacy. We believe in transparency about what information we collect and how we use it.

We do NOT collect or store your images: All image processing happens locally in your browser. Your images never leave your device and are never uploaded to our servers.

Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how our website is used, which helps us improve our service. This includes information about your browser, device, and how you interact with our site.

How We Use Information

  • To provide and improve our image processing tools
  • To analyze website usage and improve user experience
  • To ensure the security and proper functioning of our services
  • To comply with legal obligations

Data Security

Since your images never leave your device, they are not subject to our data security measures. However, we take the security of our website and services seriously.

We use industry-standard security measures to protect our website and the limited information we do collect (such as analytics data).

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: For website analytics and usage statistics
  • Google Fonts: For web fonts to improve readability

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@imagenerd.in.
